ADMISSIONS (Most Asked)
================================================================================

• When is the last date for UG/PG/XI admission 2025-26?
  → UG: Registration 10 June – 17 June 2025 (2 PM) | Spot admission 24 June 2025 (closed now)
  → PG: Registration 10 June – 23 June 2025 | Spot 2 July 2025 | Classes started 3 July 2025 (closed)
  → XI (11th): Centralized CAP process – Round 1 June 2025, Zero Round for quota 9–11 June 2025 (closed)
  Link: ugadmadv2526.pdf | pgadmadv2526.pdf | admadvxi2526.pdf

• What is the eligibility for B.Sc. / B.Com. / M.Sc.?
  → Not listed in admission ads (refer full Prospectus_25_26.pdf or SRTM University rules). 
  General: 12th pass for UG; relevant UG degree for PG. Merit-based.

  - B.A. English Medium: 100*
  - B.Com.: 320 + 100*
  - B.Sc.: 320 + 100*
  - B.Sc. Computer Science: 160*
  - B.Sc. Biotechnology: 80*
  - B.Sc. Renewable Energy: 80*
  PG (from pgadmadv2526.pdf):
  - M.A. Economics: 80
  - M.A. History: 80 + 80*
  - M.Com.: 160*
  - M.Sc. Chemistry: 12 + 28*
  - M.Sc. Zoology: 12 + 48*
  (* = Non-Grant / Self-financed)

• What documents do I need for admission?
  UG: 12th marks memo, Caste certificate, Photo, Signature (upload), ABC ID, Email, Mobile, Aadhaar (ugadmadv2526.pdf)
  PG: UG marksheets (I/II/III year), Caste + Non-Creamy Layer, ABC ID, Email, WhatsApp no., Aadhaar (pgadmadv2526.pdf)
  XI: Follow Maharashtra centralized portal (mahafyjcadmissions.in)

• Is there a management quota / spot admission?
  → Yes – Spot admission if seats remain (UG: 24 June 2025 | PG: 2 July 2025)
  → Zero Round for Management/In-House/Minority quota in XI admissions.
