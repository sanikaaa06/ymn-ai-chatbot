1.What courses are offered at the college?
Ans.The college offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.A,B.Com,B.Sc,M.A,M.Com,M.Sc in Science,Arts and Commerce.The college also offers Diploma and Certificate courses and PHD Programs.

2.What is the admission process?
Ans.The admission process involves filling out the online application form, submitting required documents, and meeting eligibility criteria. Some courses may require entrance exams.The admission mainly takes place on merit basis.

3.What are the eligibility criteria for different programs?
Ans.Eligibility depends on the course. Generally, students must have completed their previous qualifying examination with required marks.Some of the admissions also take place from MHT CET.

4.When does the admission process start and end?
Ans.Admissions usually begin in May/June. Update will be provided when the admissions begin.

5.How can I apply online?
Ans.You can apply online through our official website by filling out the application form and uploading required documents.

6.What is the fee structure for each course?
Ans.The fees ranges from  ₹1,200 to  ₹1.10 Lakhs depending on the course you choose.

7.Are there any scholarships available?
Ans.The college offers various scholarships like government scholarship for SC / ST / OBC / EBC / Minority students,Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Scholarship, Scholarships are also awarded to merit students.

8.How can I apply for financial aid?
Ans.You can apply for financial aid during your admission process by filling the scholarship form or by applying online on the government scholarship portal.

9.What is the academic calendar for this year?
Ans.The academic year usually starts in June/July. It is divided into two semesters (Summer ad Winter). Exams happen in Oct–Nov and Mar–Apr.

10.How can I access the syllabus for my course?
Ans.The college is affiliated with Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University.The syllabus for each course is updated on their website every year.

11.What are the exam schedules and evaluation methods?
Ans.The college conducts Internal exams and Pre Semester exams before the semester exams and the Semester exams are conducted by the university in Oct-Nov and Mar-Apr.Evaluation is based on tests, Assignments ,Seminars and Final exams.

12.Is there an attendance requirement?
Ans.The college does have attendance requirements and low attendance may make you ineligible for exams.

13.What facilities are available on campus?
Ans.The college offers various facilities like library, hostel, canteen and parking.Our campus also includes modern classrooms, labs,sports facilities, and Wi-Fi access.

14.Is hostel accommodation available?
Ans.The college offers hostel accommodation for students.

15.What are the campus timings?
Ans.The campus timings are from morning 10am to evening 5pm.

16.How can I contact the college administration?
Ans.The college administration can be contacte directly in the office or on the number provided by the college.

17.Does the college provide placement assistance?
Ans.Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that assists students with job opportunities and training.

18.Where is the college located?
Ans.Yeshwant Mahavidyalaya is located in Nanded, Maharashtra, on VIP Road in the Baba Nagar area. The campus is situated opposite the Government Polytechnic College.
